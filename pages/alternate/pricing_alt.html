Pricing FAQs

What factors determine project cost?

Project costs depend on scope complexity, number of integrations, custom development requirements, and ongoing support needs. We provide detailed quotes after an initial consultation to understand your specific requirements.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer milestone-based payment schedules for project work and monthly billing for retainer engagements. We're flexible and can work with your budgeting cycle.

What's included in post-launch support?

Post-launch support includes bug fixes, performance monitoring, user training, and technical assistance. Support duration varies by package (30-90 days included), with extended support available via retainer.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely. Many clients start with Starter packages and expand to Professional or Enterprise as they see results. We'll credit your initial investment toward larger engagements.

What's not included in these prices?

Third-party service costs (AI APIs, hosting, etc.) are typically passed through at cost. We provide transparent estimates during the proposal phase so there are no surprises.

How do you measure ROI?

We establish clear KPIs before project start, including efficiency metrics, cost savings, time savings, and revenue impact. All implementations include analytics dashboards to track these metrics in real-time.
