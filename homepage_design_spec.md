# sentAIent Homepage Architecture & Design Specification

## Design Objective
Create a modern, professional homepage that communicates sentAIent's value proposition within 3 seconds while maintaining sophisticated appeal for both technical and non-technical audiences.

## Brand Guidelines

### Colors
- **Primary Blue**: #60a9ff (CTAs, accents, links)
- **Dark Navy**: #202733 (headings, primary text)
- **White**: #ffffff (backgrounds, alternating sections)
- **Gray Scale**: #f8f9fa (light), #6c757d (medium), #343a40 (dark)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Sans-serif, bold, large scale (H1: 3.5rem desktop, 2.5rem mobile)
- **Body**: Sans-serif, regular weight, readable (1rem base, 1.5 line-height)
- **Accent**: Medium weight for emphasis

### Spacing
- **Base Unit**: 8px
- **Section Padding**: 80px vertical (desktop), 48px (mobile)
- **Container Max Width**: 1200px
- **Grid Gap**: 24px

## Homepage Section Architecture

### 1. Header & Navigation
**Purpose**: Consistent navigation across all pages
**Layout**: Fixed/sticky header with logo left, nav center/right
**Components**:
- sentAIent logo (linked to homepage)
- Main navigation: Services, Industries, About, Team, Contact
- Primary CTA button: "Get Started" or "Schedule Consultation"
- Mobile: Hamburger menu
